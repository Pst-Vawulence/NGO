Our Team

Dedicated individuals working tirelessly to advance healthcare access for all.

Home / Our Team
Leadership

Executive Leadership

Our executive team brings decades of combined experience in public health, medicine, operations, and community development to guide the foundation's strategic direction.

Dr. Amina Koroma

President & CEO

With over 20 years of leadership in global health, Dr. Koroma founded Medical Safe Charity Foundation and has grown it into a multi-region healthcare foundation. Her visionary leadership and unwavering commitment to health equity have impacted thousands of lives across West Africa and beyond.

Michael Adebayo

Chief Operating Officer

Michael oversees day-to-day operations, ensuring seamless coordination between field teams, logistics, and administrative functions. His background in nonprofit management and supply chain optimization has been instrumental in scaling our programs efficiently.

Dr. Sarah Okonkwo

Medical Director

A board-certified physician specializing in internal medicine and public health, Dr. Okonkwo leads all clinical aspects of our medical missions. She develops treatment protocols, oversees quality of care, and mentors the medical teams deployed across our program sites.

James Osei

Director of Programs

James is responsible for designing, implementing, and evaluating all foundation programs. With a background in international development and community engagement, he ensures each initiative is culturally sensitive, evidence-based, and aligned with community needs.

Our Staff

Program Coordinators

Our dedicated program coordinators work tirelessly on the ground to ensure every mission, workshop, and initiative runs smoothly and achieves maximum impact.

Grace Mwangi

Community Outreach Coordinator

Grace leads our community engagement efforts, building trust and partnerships with local leaders, schools, and health centers. She organizes health fairs, awareness campaigns, and patient registration for medical missions across rural and peri-urban communities.

David Ochieng

Field Operations Manager

David manages the logistics of our field missions, including transportation, equipment inventory, supply chain, and volunteer coordination. His meticulous planning ensures that every medical team arrives safely and fully equipped to serve communities in need.

Fatima Diallo

Health Education Specialist

Fatima develops and delivers culturally adapted health education curricula covering nutrition, maternal health, sanitation, disease prevention, and first aid. She trains community health workers to sustain these educational programs long after our teams depart.

Volunteers

Key Volunteers

Our volunteers are the heart of Medical Safe Charity Foundation. These dedicated individuals generously donate their time and expertise to advance our mission of healthcare for all.

Dr. James Wilson

Volunteer Physician

Dr. Wilson has volunteered on over 12 medical missions with Medical Safe Charity Foundation, providing primary care, surgical support, and telemedicine consultations. He also trains local healthcare staff and contributes to our clinical protocol development.

Priya Sharma

Volunteer Nurse

Priya is a registered nurse who brings compassion and expertise to every mission. She specializes in maternal and child health, conducting prenatal screenings, immunization drives, and health education sessions for mothers and caregivers.

Robert Kimani

Logistics Volunteer

Robert coordinates supply chain and equipment logistics for our field missions, ensuring that medical supplies, medications, and equipment are properly packed, transported, and distributed to each program location on time and in good condition.

Medical Safe Charity Foundation provides community-based healthcare for patients with diabetes, hypertension and chronic conditions — regardless of background.
