The Reality

Chronic disease is silent. Skipping a pill shouldn't cost a life.

Diabetes and hypertension don't make headlines, but they quietly take millions of lives every year — disproportionately among people who can't afford monthly medication, glucose strips, or a single doctor's visit.

1 in 10 adults
lives with diabetes — most undiagnosed in low-income areas.
1.3 billion
people have hypertension; nearly half don't know it.
Out-of-pocket cost
is the #1 reason patients abandon chronic-disease treatment.
Our Approach

Three layers of care, working together.

We don't run one program — we build a system around the patient so care doesn't stop when the camp packs up.

01

Community Clinics

Free screening, follow-up and education in the neighborhoods we serve.

02

Financial Aid

Direct subsidy of medication, lab tests and consultations for vetted patients.

03

Technology First

SMS reminders, remote monitoring and a patient registry that scales.
