About MSCF

Care without conditions. Care without exception.

We exist to make chronic disease management accessible — for the people most often left out of it.

Our Story

Started by clinicians who saw the gap firsthand.

Medical Safe Charity Foundation was founded by a group of physicians, public-health workers and technologists who kept watching the same story repeat: a patient diagnosed with diabetes or hypertension, given a prescription, then lost to follow-up because they couldn't afford the next visit.

We built MSCF as the bridge: free screening, subsidized medication, follow-up by phone, and a transparent finance model that donors can actually trust.

Mission

Make chronic disease care accessible to people who can't afford private healthcare.

Vision

A world where no one dies from a manageable condition because they couldn't pay.

Values

Humanity Over Religion. Dignity, transparency, and follow-through in every interaction.

What guides us

Our principles in practice.

Transparency

Quarterly audited reports. Every donor can trace impact.

Community first

Programs designed with local health workers, not for them.

Evidence based

Protocols based on WHO and national clinical guidelines.

Patient dignity

No paperwork that humiliates. No questions about belief.
